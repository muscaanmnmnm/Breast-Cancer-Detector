Prevention & Key Facts

While not all breast cancers can be prevented, you can take steps to lower your risk. Here are some key facts and tips.

Early Detection is Key

When caught in its earliest, localized stage, the 5-year relative survival rate is 99%.

Regular Exercise

Studies show that moderate to vigorous physical activity is linked to a lower risk of breast cancer.

Healthy Diet

A diet rich in vegetables, fruit, and whole grains can help maintain a healthy weight and lower risk.

Know Your Normal

Perform monthly self-examinations to become familiar with your breasts so you can notice any changes.
